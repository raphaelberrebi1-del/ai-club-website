AI Explorers Program

Designed for young learners who discover AI through games, creative activities, and hands-on projects. Every concept is taught through play and visual learning with immediate, exciting results.

12-Week Learning Journey:
Weeks 1-2
Meet Your AI Friend

Discover smart computers all around us!

Activity: AI Scavenger Hunt in everyday items
Weeks 3-4
Teaching AI to Understand

Learn how to talk to smart computers!

Activity: AI Pet Training with commands
Weeks 5-6
AI Art & Story Studio

Create amazing art and stories with AI!

Activity: Collaborative Art projects and Digital Collage Creation
Weeks 7-8
AI Helpers in Our World

Discover AI helpers everywhere!

Activity: Career Dress-Up with AI jobs and AI Assistant design
Weeks 9-10
Smart Choices with AI

Learn when to use AI and when to use our brains!

Activity: Choice Champions scenarios and Privacy Protector Game
Weeks 11-12
Young Innovator Showcase

Create and present amazing AI projects!

Activity: Choose Your Adventure project and Family Showcase Event
What Your Child Will Achieve:
  • Personal AI art gallery to share with family
  • AI Helpers book they created themselves
  • Portfolio of creative projects and worksheets
  • Confidence presenting to family and friends
AI Mastery Program

Designed for middle schoolers ready to dive deeper into AI technology. Students explore real-world applications, develop professional skills, and build impressive portfolios while discovering AI career pathways.

12-Week Learning Journey:
Weeks 1-2
AI Career Explorer

Industry landscape tour, AI jobs of the future, and setting career goals

Activity: Create My AI Future vision board and career roadmap
Weeks 3-4
Prompt Engineering Pro

Advanced prompting techniques, chain-of-thought reasoning, and optimization strategies

Activity: Build custom AI assistant for homework help
Weeks 5-6
School Success Hacker

AI tools for research, note-taking, studying, and project organization

Activity: Transform a school project using AI research and presentation tools
Weeks 7-8
Content Creator Accelerator

AI-powered writing, design, video, and social media content creation

Activity: Launch personal blog or YouTube channel with AI-assisted content
Weeks 9-10
Responsible AI Leader

AI ethics, bias detection, privacy concerns, and developing responsible usage habits

Activity: Create AI ethics guide for peers and present to school community
Weeks 11-12
Capstone Portfolio Project

Comprehensive project showcasing all learned skills with professional presentation

Activity: Choose from: AI-enhanced science fair project, startup pitch, or social impact initiative
What Your Teen Will Achieve:
  • Professional AI toolkit for academic and creative success
  • Career clarity with concrete next steps in AI field
  • Published content showcasing AI collaboration skills
  • Leadership experience in AI ethics and responsible innovation
  • Impressive portfolio project for high school applications
AI Leadership Academy

Designed for high school students ready to become AI leaders and innovators. This intensive program develops entrepreneurial thinking, professional skills, and real-world business applications that prepare students for college and careers.

12-Week Leadership Journey:
Weeks 1-2
AI Industry Pioneer

Deep dive into AI market analysis, emerging opportunities, and competitive landscape assessment

Activity: Create comprehensive AI industry report and investment thesis presentation
Weeks 3-4
Professional AI Engineer

Advanced prompt engineering, API integration, and workflow automation for professional environments

Activity: Build and deploy custom AI solution for real client or school challenge
Weeks 5-6
Business Automation Expert

Enterprise AI applications, process optimization, cost-benefit analysis, and ROI calculation

Activity: Audit local business and create comprehensive AI transformation plan
Weeks 7-8
Marketing & Brand Strategist

AI-powered marketing campaigns, brand development, content strategy, and customer experience design

Activity: Launch complete marketing campaign for startup idea or local organization
Weeks 9-10
Ethics & Policy Leader

AI governance frameworks, regulatory compliance, bias mitigation, and ethical decision-making in business

Activity: Draft AI policy proposal for school district or local government
Weeks 11-12
Capstone: Startup Launch

Comprehensive business plan, financial projections, and investor pitch for AI-powered venture

Activity: Present to real investors/entrepreneurs and receive professional feedback
What Your Teen Will Achieve:
  • Professional-grade AI development and deployment skills
  • Real business experience with measurable impact and testimonials
  • Startup pitch deck and business plan ready for investors
  • Leadership portfolio demonstrating ethics and policy expertise
  • College application standout with entrepreneurship credentials
  • Network of mentors and industry connections
